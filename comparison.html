The Price of Convenience vs. Cooking

We all love the convenience of grabbing a burrito bowl or a pasta dish from a restaurant, but those costs add up quickly. By cooking at home, you are paying for the food, not the labor, rent, and profit margin of the restaurant.

The table below compares the average cost of popular student meals when purchased at a restaurant versus the cost of ingredients to make them at home.

Average Cost per Serving (2026 Prices)
Meal Item Restaurant Price Home-Cooked Price Your Savings
Chicken Burrito Bowl (e.g., Chipotle) $11.50 $3.50 $8.00
Pasta with Meat Sauce (e.g., Olive Garden) $16.00 $4.25 $11.75
Gourmet Burger & Fries $14.00 $5.00 $9.00
Avocado Toast & Coffee $12.50 $2.75 $9.75

What Could You Do With the Savings?

If you swap just three meals a week from restaurant to homemade, you could save nearly $120 a month. That covers a streaming subscription, a gym membership, and your phone bill!
